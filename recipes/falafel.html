Falafel

Description

Falafel is an all-time favorite street food. In most parts of the Middle East, falafel is made with ground chickpeas. They are best served with pita bread, tomato, onions, and tahini sauce.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups dried split fava beans
  • 1 red onion, quartered
  • ½ cup fresh parsley
  • ½ cup fresh cilantro
  • ½ cup fresh dill
  • 3 cloves garlic
  • 1½ teaspoons ground coriander
  • 1½ teaspoons salt
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1 cup sesame seeds (optional)
  • vegetable oil for frying
